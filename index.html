About me

I am a 29 year old bay area native specializing in photography and web design. I started as a staff writer and photojournalist for the college newspaper The Advocate in Contra Costa College and graduated in March of 2020 with an Associates Degree in Mass Communications.

While still in college I began doing freelance photography then after graduating I started a clothing brand and am now venturing into web development. Below you can find some of my journalism work, photography work and my clothing brand. This page was developed and programmed by me as well.
